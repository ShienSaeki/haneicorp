VOC Treatment Systems

HANEI provides a full range of VOC abatement systems tailored for various industries. Our solutions are energy-efficient, regulatory-compliant, and built for operational durability.

Regenerative Thermal Oxidizer (RTO)

High-efficiency combustion technology for VOC destruction (>99%), featuring ceramic heat exchangers and automatic temperature control. Ideal for electronics and chemical manufacturing.

Carbon Adsorption System

Utilizes activated carbon to capture low-concentration VOCs. Equipped with auto-desorption modules for cyclic operation and reusable carbon media.

Rotor Concentrator + Thermal Oxidizer

Combines a zeolite rotor wheel to condense high air volumes into smaller VOC streams, followed by thermal oxidation. Suited for LCD and printing industries.

Wet Scrubber Systems

Water-based scrubbing towers designed to neutralize acid gases, soluble VOCs, and fine particles. Available in FRP or stainless steel designs.
